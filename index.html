The III Project

The III Project is a project where you can create images with the twist by adding "III" to the middle and share it on the Discord server where the community can take a look at your creation.

On Sep 5 2015, the YouTube channel Denovali Records uploaded a video titled "Bersarin Quartett - III (Trailer)", this video featured the Genesis III. This is the Sole Reason we have created this society and we hope that you enjoy our journey together where we shall grow into a family and become an unstoppable force in this day and age.
